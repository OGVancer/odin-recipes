Fetuccini Alfredo

Home

Recipe Description

Noodles, butter, flour, garlic, milk, hot sauce, and parmesan cheese. If you like thinner noodles, use linguine noodles.

Ingridients:

  • 8 ounces dry fettuccine pasta
  • 4 ½ tablespoons butter
  • 2 ½ tablespoons all-purpose flour
  • 2 cups of milk
  • 1 tea spoon monced garlic
  • 5 drops hot pepper sauce
  • ½ cup grated Parmesan cheese

Directions:

  1. Cook pasta in a large pot of boiling water
  2. Meanwhile, melt butter or margarine in a saucepan. Stir in flour. Whisk in milk,garlic, and hot sauce; stir until thckened. Stir in cheese.
  3. Drain pasta. Serve sauce over noodles.
